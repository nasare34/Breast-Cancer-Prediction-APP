Understanding Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women. It occurs when the cells in the breast grow out of control. Early detection through screening and regular check-ups can significantly improve survival rates. It's essential to be aware of the risk factors, symptoms, and available treatments.

Women are encouraged to perform regular breast self-examinations and participate in mammography screenings as recommended by their healthcare provider. If you notice any changes in your breasts, such as lumps, changes in size, or unusual discharge, seek medical advice promptly.
